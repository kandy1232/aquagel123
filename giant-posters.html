Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order a giant coloring poster? +
Simply click the "Enquire on WhatsApp" button on any product or size card and send us a message. Our team will guide you through the ordering process, confirm availability and arrange delivery.
Can I get a custom design for my school event? +
Yes! We offer custom giant poster designs for schools, events and organisations. Contact us on WhatsApp with your requirements including theme, size preference, and event date, and our design team will create something special for you.
How are the giant posters delivered? +
All giant posters are carefully rolled and placed in protective tubes for safe transportation. We offer island-wide delivery across Sri Lanka. Delivery time is typically 2–5 business days depending on your location.
What type of markers or paints can I use on the posters? +
Our giant posters work with most coloring media including markers, crayons, colored pencils, watercolor paints and poster paints. We recommend water-based markers for best results on the large sizes.
Are bulk order discounts available? +
Absolutely! We offer special pricing for schools and organisations ordering multiple posters. Contact us on WhatsApp or email for bulk order quotes and school partnership rates.
What educational topics are available for giant posters? +
Our giant coloring posters cover all 6 Aquagel educational categories: World & Sri Lanka Maps, Animals & Insects, Solar System & Planets, Human Body & Parts, and more. New designs are added regularly.
